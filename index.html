From the beginning, the inspiration for our island parks has been to create a tropical oasis with a sense of far away, exotic journey, thrilling attractions, and world-class fun for families in the Midwest.

Waterpark Dining Options

Castaway Cafe

The tempting smell from our outdoor grill permeates the Island with grilled burgers, hot dogs, chicken and more. Also choose from juicy chicken tender bites and crispy fries or warm pretzels to snack on. There's something for every appetite!

High Tide Pizza

Check out our new High Tide Pizza! We are serving up sizzling hot pizza with all your favorite toppings! Buy it by the slice or share a 14" with your friends and family. Maybe you want to add a zesty side salad to your meal. Mix and match flavors to satisfy every craving!

Treetop Cafe

Join us at the Treetop Cafe for lunch and enjoy a spectacular view overlooking the park. Here you will find your staple snacks like chicken bites, tacos, and nachos. Or you might want to try our tender and delicious pulled pork sandwich. Get it while it's hot!
