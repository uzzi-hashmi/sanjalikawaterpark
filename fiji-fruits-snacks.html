Fiji Fruits Snacks

Fiji Fruits & Snacks is the perfect place to chill out! Meet your friends for an afternoon snack. This hut is located at the base of Tangerine Tempest, and is home to our famous Dippin’ Dots, tasty pineapple Dole Whip, funnel cakes, shaved ice, and other one-of-a-kind treats!
