Cyclone Pool

Welcome and join us for a wet and wild fun in our Cyclone Pool. Swim and splash in the pool, designed for the guests to relax and enjoy on a quite environment. Make a day of it. There is fun for everyone in The Sanjalika Water Park. Beat the Heat with moderately cold water. A perfect place, to get relief from Heat, and having Fun at the same time. Take a dip, sit back, relax and let out a sigh, because life is too short to enjoy and nice to be sitting on the shore like environments with liberty to play with water. First moments inside Cyclone pool almost, always jar your senses. A rush of moist, chlorine-scented air fills your nose. And then there is continuous loop of nonstop latest popular music vying with an endless whir of water. This is Cyclone Pool.
